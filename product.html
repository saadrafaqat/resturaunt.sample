A5 Wagyu Tataki

$185

Our signature A5 Wagyu Tataki celebrates the exquisite marbling of Kobe beef — sourced directly from a single farm in Hyōgo Prefecture. The beef is flash-seared at 800°F for exactly 12 seconds, preserving the delicate fat structure.

Served with aged ponzu, micro shiso, and shaved black truffles. Each plate is a meditation on texture, temperature, and taste — designed to showcase the pure essence of umami.

Key Ingredients

  • A5 Wagyu Beef (Kobe)
  • Aged Ponzu (6 months)
  • Périgord Black Truffle
  • Micro Shiso Leaves
  • Wasabi Root (fresh grated)
  • Himalayan Pink Salt
  • Sesame Oil (cold-pressed)
  • Edible Gold Leaf
