Our Philosophy

The Art of Umami

At UmamiQuest, we believe in the power of the fifth taste — the deep, savory essence that transforms a meal into an experience. Our kitchen is led by Chef Hiroshi Tanaka, who brings 30 years of mastery to every plate.

We source the finest ingredients from around the globe: A5 Wagyu from Kobe, black truffles from Périgord, and seafood delivered fresh from Tsukiji each morning.
